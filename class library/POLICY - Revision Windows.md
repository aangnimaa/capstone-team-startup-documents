# POLICY — Revision Windows

## Purpose

Revision windows allow students to **improve previously submitted work**.

They are designed to support learning and improvement while keeping grading fair and consistent.

Revision windows are **not the same as late work**.

---

# Key Rule

Revisions are **improvement‑only**.

Missed work still receives a **zero**.

However, the work must still be completed so the team can continue progressing.

---

# What Can Be Revised

Typical items that may be revised include:

- Sprint Packet quality
- documentation
- demos
- evidence / receipts

The instructor will specify **which items are eligible** during each revision window.

---

# What Cannot Be Revised

These items normally cannot be revised:

- attendance
- missed presentations
- work never submitted

---

# How Revisions Are Submitted

Revisions must show **clear improvement**.

Students must provide:

- a link to the original work
- a link to the improved version
- a short explanation of what changed

Evidence may include:

- updated documentation
- improved demo
- additional testing
- clearer project scope

---

# Revision Deadlines

Revision windows will be announced during the semester.

They are typically **short and time‑limited**.

Work submitted after the window closes will not be reconsidered.

---

# Example Revision

Original Sprint Packet:

- unclear demo
- missing receipts

Improved submission:

- new demo video
- corrected receipts
- clearer sprint notes

---

# Why Revision Windows Exist

The goal is to reward:

- learning
- improvement
- better engineering practice

Not simply turning in work late.
